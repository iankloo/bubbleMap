COVID-19 Infectious Probability by County

This visualization represents each county in the United States with a single circle. The radius of each circle is based on the infectious probability in that county. Use the time slider at the bottom to explore the changes in infectious probability over the course of the pandemic.

Unlike a typical map visualization, this methodology sacrifices geographical integrity for a more intuitive view of how the pandemic has changed over time. For example, some circles may become so large that they crowd-out surrounding counties. The idea of this visualization is not to make it easy to find exactly what is happening in individual counties (that is the strength of Bigmap). Instead, the idea here is to explore the general trends and flows across the entire country.
